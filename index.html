Empowering AI with Deeper Insights

SHEVA 2.0 acts as a discovery engine to augment Large Language Models (LLMs), transforming them into powerful analytical partners.

The Challenge with LLMs

While LLMs excel at generating high-level summaries, they often lack the specialized capability to conduct a deep, multi-conditional, and statistically rigorous exploration of tabular data. On their own, they perform a competent but superficial analysis, missing the non-obvious patterns hidden within.

The SHEVA 2.0 Solution

By providing a set of deep, statistically-vetted, and multi-conditional hypotheses, SHEVA 2.0 empowers LLMs to interpret and articulate complex patterns, interactions, and even sophisticated statistical concepts like variance, leading to richer and more reliable analytical narratives.

Go Beyond Simple Correlations

SHEVA 2.0 elevates data analysis from a descriptive task to a true engine for knowledge discovery.

Discover Complex Subgroups

Automatically identify multi-conditional rules that define specific segments (e.g., customers, products) that deviate significantly from the average.

Analyze Outcome Variance

Uncover groups where outcomes are most inconsistent. Understand not just the average behavior, but also the stability and predictability of results.

Ensure Statistical Rigor

Every hypothesis is evaluated with robust metrics like q-value (FDR), Impact (Lift), and Coverage, ensuring your insights are statistically valid and meaningful.
